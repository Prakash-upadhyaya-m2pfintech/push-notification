On the reciver tag in Android manifest where the intent filter is present
    add  android:exported="true" => to reciver tag to avoid the app crash 
    


<receiver  android:exported="true" android:name="com.dieam.reactnativepushnotification.modules.RNPushNotificationBootEventReceiver">
            <intent-filter>
                <action android:name="android.intent.action.BOOT_COMPLETED" />
                <action android:name="android.intent.action.QUICKBOOT_POWERON" />
                <action android:name="com.htc.intent.action.QUICKBOOT_POWERON"/>
            </intent-filter>
</receiver>
